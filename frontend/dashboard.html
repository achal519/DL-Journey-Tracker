🏛️ RTO Driving Test — Complete Preparation Guide
Everything you need to know before going to RTO

📦 What to Carry to RTO

  • ✅ Original Learner License (LL)
  • ✅ 2 Photocopies of LL
  • ✅ Original Age Proof (Aadhaar / Birth Certificate)
  • ✅ Original Address Proof (Aadhaar / Voter ID)
  • ✅ Filled Form 9 (Application for DL)
  • ✅ 2 Recent Passport-size Photographs
  • ✅ RTO Test Fee — ₹300 approx (cash or DD)
  • ✅ Medical Certificate Form 1A (if age above 40)

⏰ Day of Test — Hour by Hour Timeline

  • Reach RTO Office 7:30 AM
    Arrive 30 minutes before your slot. Find the DL Test counter and submit your documents to the clerk.
  • Document Verification 8:00 AM
    Officer checks your LL, Form 9, age/address proof and photos. You get a token number for your test slot.
  • Fee Payment 8:30 AM
    Pay the driving test fee at the cash counter. Keep the receipt — it is required for DL collection later.
  • Wait for Your Token 9:00 AM
    Wait in the designated area. Tests are called by token number. Average wait is 30–60 minutes depending on footfall.
  • Driving Test Begins 10:00 AM
    Inspector calls your token. Bring your vehicle to the test track. Test duration: 5–10 minutes per candidate.
  • Result Announcement 11:00 AM
    Inspector marks Pass/Fail. If passed, you submit remaining documents. DL is dispatched by post within 7–30 days.
  • DL Dispatch / Collection Within 30 days
    Your Permanent DL arrives at your registered address by Speed Post. Track it on parivahan.gov.in using your application number.

🛣️ How the Test Track Works

Standard RTO test track path (may vary slightly by RTO):
🚦
Start Point — Inspector signals you to begin. Start engine, check mirrors, wear seatbelt/helmet.
➡️
Straight Line Drive — Drive straight at controlled speed (20–30 kmph). No swerving.
🔄
Left Turn — Signal left, check mirror, slow down, take wide turn. Stop completely at stop line.
⛰️
H-Point / Slope Start (4W) — Stop on slope, apply handbrake. Start without rolling back more than 30cm.
🔢
Figure-8 (2W) — Ride figure-8 pattern at slow speed. Both feet on footrests throughout.
🅿️
Parking (4W) — Parallel park or reverse park in marked bay within 3 attempts.
🏁
End Point — Stop at marked finish line. Turn off engine. Inspector gives verdict.

🎯 Key Disciplines Inspector Checks

🪞 Mirror Usage Check rear and side mirrors before every turn, lane change, and stop. Inspector watches eye movement.
🚦 Signal Usage Indicate left/right at least 30 metres before turning. Never turn without signalling.
⚖️ Speed Control Maintain 20–30 kmph on test track. No sudden acceleration or harsh braking.
🛑 Stop Lines Come to a complete stop at every stop line. Rolling stops = automatic fail.
🦺 Safety Gear Helmet mandatory for 2W. Seatbelt mandatory for 4W. Must be worn before starting engine.
👀 Road Awareness Look ahead, not down. Watch for inspector's hand signals. Respond calmly to instructions.
🔄 Gear Shifting (4W) Shift gears smoothly without stalling. No grinding. Use clutch properly before changing gear.
📏 Lane Discipline Stay in your lane. Do not cross marked lines on track. Keep safe distance from cones/markers.

✅ Do's and ❌ Don'ts

✅ DO These

  • Greet the inspector politely
  • Adjust mirrors before starting
  • Wear helmet / seatbelt first
  • Stop completely at stop lines
  • Signal before every turn
  • Drive at steady controlled speed
  • Stay calm — breathe normally
  • Ask inspector if unclear

❌ DON'T Do These

  • Don't use mobile phone on track
  • Don't skip mirror checks
  • Don't roll back on slope (4W)
  • Don't put feet down on 2W
  • Don't brake suddenly / harshly
  • Don't argue with inspector
  • Don't stall the engine multiple times
  • Don't cross stop lines

🔄 How to Re-apply for LL
Since your LL has expired, you must start the process again from scratch:
  1. Visit your nearest RTO office or go to parivahan.gov.in
  2. Fill Form 2 — Application for Learner License
  3. Carry Age Proof, Address Proof, and 2 Passport-size Photos
  4. Pay the LL application fee (approx. ₹200)
  5. Clear the Written Theory Test at RTO (traffic signs & rules)
  6. Collect your new LL — the 30-day waiting period starts fresh
